Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about GIKI Entrance Test and Admissions

What is the eligibility criteria for GIKI admission?

+

Basic Eligibility for BS Engineering: 60% or above in Math, Physics, and Overall. Acceptable qualifications: HSSC Pre-Engineering, ICS, Pre-Medical with Additional Math, A-Levels, American/Canadian High School/IB Diploma, DAE in relevant technology. IBCC Equivalence required for foreign qualifications.

Basic Eligibility for BS Management Sciences: 60% or above overall marks. Acceptable qualifications: HSSC, A-Levels, American/Canadian High School or IB Diploma with 60%+ (IBCC).

What is the test pattern for GIKI entrance exam?

+

BS Engineering: 80 MCQs (Physics 30, Mathematics 30, English 20), 2 hours, no negative marking.

BS Management Sciences: 80 MCQs (Arithmetic & Logical Reasoning 40, English 40), 2 hours, no negative marking.

How can I prepare for the GIKI entrance test?

+

Focus on FSc/HSSC syllabus, especially Physics and Mathematics. Practice past papers, time management, and work on your speed and accuracy. Use the study materials section for resources.

Are calculators allowed in the entrance test?

+

Yes! System calculators are allowed.
